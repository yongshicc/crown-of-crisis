Crown of Crisis

A complete, lightweight historical-political mini game. Choose a protagonist route and make four decisive choices across Vienna, Pressburg, Budapest, and the Ottoman Frontier. The AI political simulation reacts through the Nobility, Church, Army, and Nationalist factions.
Included: background-removed 3D protagonists, character sprite system, dialogue avatars, expression changes, animated sprites, map, and AI political simulation. One playthrough takes about 6–10 minutes.
